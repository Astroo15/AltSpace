AltSpace is a digital space for scene, emo, alt, and internet kids. Inspired by early MySpace chaos, glitter GIFs, messy profiles, and being unapologetically yourself.

What is Scene / Alt?

Scene and alt styles are about expression, emotion, color, contrast, and community. Bright hair next to black eyeliner energy. Cute + aggressive. Loud visuals, honest feelings.

AltSpace exists to bring back that energy — profiles you can customize, blogs you actually control, and a place that doesn’t force you to be “clean” or “minimal”.

What You Can Do Here

  • Create a customizable profile (colors, bio, images)
  • Write blog posts like old-school MySpace
  • Express your aesthetic without filters
  • Exist loudly, quietly, or chaotically
